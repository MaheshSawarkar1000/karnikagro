Who We Are

Rooted in Trust, Growing Together

As a registered Farm Producer Company (FPC), Karnika Agro bridges the gap between scientific agricultural research and ground-level farming operations. We are built for farmers, by farmers.

Quality Assurance

We source and test every seed, fertilizer, and protective product to ensure optimal germination and yield stability for our farmer shareholder members.

Farmer Ownership

Our FPC model ensures profits are reinvested into local farming communities, giving farmers direct equity and voice in agricultural distribution.

Market Reach

We remove intermediaries by establishing direct supply networks to major food retailers and wholesale markets, securing higher prices for farmer produce.

Our Core Ecosystem

Sales & Services on Both Sides

We support the entire agricultural cycle. We supply premium farming inputs (Sales) and offer expert tech-driven farm care, concluding with direct market buybacks (Services).

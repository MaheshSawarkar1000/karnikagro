# Karnika Gro - Livestock & Cattle Protection Guide

## Overview
This repository contains comprehensive information about **Cattle Protection and Livestock Management** (वीज पडण्याचा पशूंचे संरक्षण) with focus on securing cattle from natural disasters and ensuring sustainable farming practices.

## Key Topics Covered

### 1. Main Objectives (मुख्य उद्दिष्टे)
- **Livestock Safety Protocol**: Comprehensive safeguarding measures for cattle from hurricanes, hailstorms, and natural calamities
- **Protective Infrastructure**: Building proper shelters and safe housing for livestock
- **Disaster Management**: Emergency protocols and preventive measures

### 2. Lightning & Storm Protection (वीज संरक्षण)
- Understanding lightning hazards for cattle
- Identifying vulnerable zones during thunderstorms
- Implementing lightning protection systems
- Emergency shelter procedures during severe weather

### 3. Preventive & Therapeutic Methods
- **Housing Solutions**: Proper barn construction and shelter design
- **Community Support**: Group training and awareness programs
- **Medical Care**: Veterinary assistance and health monitoring
- **Insurance**: Livestock protection schemes and coverage

### 4. Cattle Breeds & Characteristics
- Local breed identification and care
- Understanding breed-specific vulnerabilities
- Maintenance and feeding practices for different breeds

### 5. Do's & Don'ts (काय करावे व काय करू नये)
- ✅ **Do's**: Proper feeding, timely veterinary care, safe housing, regular health checks
- ❌ **Don'ts**: Avoid inadequate shelter, negligent care, and risky practices
